## Refined Topic

A comprehensive survey on ultra-low bit quantization (1-bit to 4-bit) for large language models (LLMs), covering both post-training quantization (PTQ) and quantization-aware training (QAT) paradigms. The survey systematically reviews algorithm innovations, benchmark evaluation protocols, theoretical foundations, and identifies research gaps and future directions in LLM model compression.
